Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is SignScape?

SignScape is an online platform dedicated to sign language education
and promotion. It offers a variety of resources including tutorials,
and games to facilitate learning and engagement with sign language.

Who is SignScape for?

SignScape is for anyone interested in learning sign language,
whether you're a beginner or an advanced signer.
It's also a valuable resource for educators, parents,
and individuals working with the deaf and hard of hearing community.

Is SignScape accessible to people with disabilities?

Yes, accessibility is a priority for SignScape.
The platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to individuals
of all abilities, including those with disabilities.
